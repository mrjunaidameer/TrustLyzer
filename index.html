How the Scan Works (Simulated Heuristics)

Trustlyzer analyzes multiple technical indicators (heuristics) to calculate a risk score, mimicking commercial services. We check for HTTPS presence, suspicious Top-Level Domains (TLDs), potential Typosquatting, Domain Age, and Simulated Geo-IP Location to provide an immediate security assessment. This approach helps identify patterns common in phishing and scam attempts.

This tool is intended for educational purposes, highlighting how to identify common red flags in suspicious URLs and promoting a fearlessly secure browsing experience.
